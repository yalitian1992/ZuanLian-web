Zuanlian Technology was founded in 2016. With the revolutionary diamond financial engine and the world's first "diamond matrix" which standardizes complex varieties of diamonds, Zuanlian Technology turns diamonds from the traditional jewelry into a unique new asset class---a portable, with fair prices, store and appreciation of value and highly liquid assets. From then on, diamond trade flows and financial flows will be the most efficiently connected, users will be able to have safe and efficient trade diamond assets.
